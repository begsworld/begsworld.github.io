Privacy Policy for CalDiary<br>
Last updated: May 6, 2026<br>
CalDiary is a private, local photo diary app. This privacy policy explains how your data is handled.<br>
Data Collection<br>
CalDiary does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All photos and notes you add to the app remain entirely on your device.<br>
Third Parties<br>
CalDiary does not share any information with third parties. There are no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no external servers.
Photos<br>
CalDiary accesses your photo library only to display and save photos you explicitly choose. Your photos are never uploaded anywhere.<br>
Changes<br>
If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.<br>
