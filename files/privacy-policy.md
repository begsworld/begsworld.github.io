# Privacy Policy for CalDiary 
## Last updated: May 6, 2026 

CalDiary is a private, local photo diary app. This privacy policy explains how your data is handled. 
 
## Data Collection 
CalDiary does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All photos and notes you add to the app remain entirely on your device. 
 
## Third Parties 
CalDiary does not share any information with third parties. There are no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no external servers. 
 
## Photos 
CalDiary accesses your photo library only to display and save photos you explicitly choose. Your photos are never uploaded anywhere. 
 
## Changes 
If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
